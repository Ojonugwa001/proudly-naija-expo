Construction of Stand

It is the intention of the organizers to have a uniform pavilion/stand. Therefore exhibitors are expected to patronize only accredited stand contractors who have got standard charge per square meter for the construction of stand.

Please note that exhibitors who construct stands using more space than the area paid for and allocated to them shall be surcharged for the additional space at the double the normal rate for such space. Contravention notice and debit notes shall be served on such organization and the Organizers shall seize the company's property commensurate with the amount being owed as a guarantee for the payment of the stipulated fine. Upon service of contravention notice and debit notes for such surcharge, contravening party would be expected to pay within a period of 48 hours, failure of which the organizers shall be free to dispose of the seized items.

For more information on construction of stands and any other aspect of the Expo, kindly contact us at: info@proudlynigeriaexpo.org
