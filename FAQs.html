Frequently asked questions

General

What Is Nigeria Expo?expand_more

Nigeria Expo is an online learning community where anyone can discover, take, or even teach a class. Anyone can join Tutstack to start watching online classes, create projects, and even become a teacher. We started Nigeria Expo to close the professional skills gap and provide universal access to high-quality learning. By teaching the skills needed in tomorrow's world, Nigeria Expo empowers people to advance their careers, improve their lives, and pursue the work they love.
